About The Recommender

The recommender system compares your survey answers with text from each programâ€™s website. After calculating a similarity score for content and tone, it presents a ranked list. About Me

My name is Kendall Berry, I am a Physics PhD with a research background in computational neuroscience. I had CFS for a total of 3 years before fully recovering (read more about me and my recovery story here). Brain retraining was crucial to my recovery, and this project is my way of giving back to the ME/CFS community.
