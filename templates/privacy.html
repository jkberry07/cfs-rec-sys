Privacy Statement

What Information Do You Collect?

I do not collect any personal identifying information about you. The only data I store is:

  • Which survey questions you choose to answer and your filter settings.
  • The ranking of recommended programs.
  • Specific program text sentences that match with your survey answers.
  • Which program website links you click on (but nothing about your activity on those websites).

    • If you choose to opt in, your survey answers are stored anonymously to help improve the recommendation system and provide insights to programs about what users are looking for.

    I also do not use:

  • Cookies
  • Analytics or user tracking services
  • Third-party data collection tools

    • How Do You Use This Information?

    The data I store is used solely for:

  • Quality assurance and improvement of the recommendation system
  • Reporting to programs how many users visit their websites from this site
  • Sharing with programs which types of content most closely align with users' needs.

    • For more about how the site works, see the About page.

    If you have questions about this privacy statement, please contact me.

    Disclaimer: The content in and throughout this website is for informational and educational purposes only, and is not a substitute for medical diagnosis, treatment or advice.
