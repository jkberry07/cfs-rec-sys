Privacy Statement

What Information Do You Collect?

I do not collect any personal identifying information about you. The only data I store is:

  • Which survey questions you choose to answer
  • Your top 5 recommended programs based on your responses
  • Specific program text sentences that match with your survey answers
  • Which program website links you click on (but nothing about your activity on those websites)

    • I do not save your responses to the questions.

    I also do not use:

  • Cookies
  • Analytics or user tracking services
  • Third-party data collection tools

    • What Link Click Information Do You Store?

    I log when you click on program website links to understand which recommended programs are most interesting to users. This helps me evaluate and improve the recommendation system. I only track the click itself - I do not monitor your activity once you visit the program websites.

    How Do You Use This Information?

    The data I store is used solely for:

  • Quality assurance of the recommendation system
  • Improving and refining future versions of the recommendation system
  • Reporting to programs how many users visit their websites from this site
  • Sharing with programs which types of content most closely match with users' needs

    • For more about how the site works, see the About page.

    If you have questions about this privacy statement, please contact me.
